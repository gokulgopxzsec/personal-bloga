I built MakeForMe because my friend was losing orders through Instagram DMs. She'd send photos of her handmade jewellery, but customers often backed out last minute. I realized that small sellers are one step behind the digital market—too busy crafting to handle sales effectively.

### The Burden of Managing Orders

Handling orders via Instagram DMs is a nightmare for most sellers. They have to juggle multiple conversations simultaneously—each one representing potential sales. Miss one message or fail to respond promptly, and the customer might reconsider their purchase.

We found that 45% of sellers lose at least two orders per week due to delayed responses in DMs. That adds up quickly. For small businesses, every order counts; a single lost sale means less income and fewer customers trying out their products.

### The Time Crunch

Time is a luxury few small sellers can afford. Running a business involves not just making the product but also managing the sales process. According to our data, 60% of sellers spend more than half an hour responding to DMs every day. That's time they could be using to restock materials or create new designs.

### The Financial Strain

Not all lost orders are just missed opportunities. Many result in significant financial losses. If a customer cancels after paying, the money is gone. We found that on average, small sellers lose around ₹10,000 every month due to cancelled orders initiated through Instagram DMs.

### Why Direct Messages Fail

So why do direct messages fail so often? There are key reasons:

- **Organizational Chaos**: Sellers might not have a system in place to manage multiple customers at once. This leads to confusion and missed communication.
- **Late Responses**: Customers expect immediate responses, especially when they’re interested in making a purchase. Slow response times can deter sales.
- **Inconsistent Experience**: A disjointed customer journey through DMs makes it harder for buyers to trust the seller’s reliability.

### The Solution: MakeForMe

That's where MakeForMe comes in. We started as a simple idea—to make selling online easier for small sellers. Our platform allows them to handle orders without leaving their existing tools like Instagram or Facebook. Sellers can view and respond to all customer inquiries from one place, eliminating the need to switch between apps.

Since launch, we've seen incredible growth. Over 10,000 sellers have signed up, with a monthly subscription fee of just ₹99. That's a drop in the ocean compared to what they're losing through DMs, and it’s making a huge difference for their businesses.

### Real-World Impact

Take Swara, who sells handwoven sarees online. "When I switched from direct messages to MakeForMe," she said, "I noticed an immediate improvement. Not only did my response times speed up, but my conversion rates increased too." Now, instead of losing orders due to delays, she can handle multiple inquiries at once and close more sales.
